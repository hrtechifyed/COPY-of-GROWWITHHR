PRIVACY

Privacy principles aligned to modern data protection expectations.

Assessment responses are used to generate the advisory in the browser session. The tool is designed around data minimisation, purpose limitation and transparency principles aligned to GDPR-style privacy expectations and other applicable privacy laws.

  • Collect only information needed for the advisory.
  • Avoid selling visitor data.
  • Use email/name capture only to identify the advisory recipient.
  • Encourage users not to enter confidential personal employee data.
